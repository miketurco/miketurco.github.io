Emergency/Prepper 11/06/16

What to do when a diabetic passes out?

When a diabetic passes out, call 911. It's either because their blood sugar is too high or too low. Do NOT give them insulin!

Be a hero: buy and learn how to use these tools: glucose tabs, a BG meter, a glucagon gun and keto strips.
Emergency/Prepper 11/05/16

Is your insulin ready to go?

Keep insulin in the fridge, inside a diabetes bag, along with some syringes. That way if you must bug out in an emergency, you can grab the bag and be ready to go.

It would be a good idea to keep hard candy in the bag as well.
