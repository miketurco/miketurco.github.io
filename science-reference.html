    What is a C-Peptide test?

    C-peptide lab test results tell you how much insulin your pancreas is producing.

    T1's produce no insulin at all. T2's often produce a lot, which can lead to heart disease and other complications.

    Correlation of A1c to Cardiovascular Disease

    CVD refers to heart conditions such as diseased vessels, structural problems and blood clots.

    An A1c level of >5% puts you at an increased level of risk for CVD.

    Science/Reference 11/11/16

    Normal blood sugar levels of non-diabetic individuals

    CGM's (Constant Glucose Monitors) were attached to a group of 74 non-diabetic individuals, ages 9 to 65.

    Their average A1C was 5.3. Fasting BG ranged from 78 to 94. Readings were much the same across age groups.

    Parts of a syringe

    The basic parts of a syringe are the barrel, plunger, and tip.

    Needles have three parts: the hub, the shaft and the bevel. The hollow hole that goes through the needle is called a lumen.
