Grapefruit may be deadly mix with some meds

Grapefruit can block enzymes that slow down the body's absoptoin of medications. This can spell bad news.

Best advice: don't eat grapefruit, find another source of vitamin C.

Best Size for Needles?

Longer needles may hurt less, and the same goes for thicker needles.

The best size for needle is the one that works best for you. It's a good idea to experiment with different gauges and lengths.

Minimize the Burn from Injections

A small drop of insulin at the tip of the needle can create a burning sensation when the skin is first punctured.

Flick the drip off the tip before you inject. It may not eliminate the sensation, but it does help.
