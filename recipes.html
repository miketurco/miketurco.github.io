    Butter Tea

    Butter tea is what it sounds like: butter and tea.

    Your best bet is with black tea and quality, grass fed butter. Sweeten as needed. One tablespoon of butter per cup gives you a 100-calorie drink.

    It's a different taste and takes a little getting used to. It's very filling, though.

    Medium heat. Chop them up as you cook to make them fluffy.
